What is Quizify?
Quizify is a platform where you can create quizzes, flashcards, and fun “Do You Know Me?” tests to challenge your friends.
Do I need an account to use Quizify?
Yes you require account to explore features using your account, creating quizzes and saving progress requires login.
Who can use Quizify?
Anyone! Students, friends, or anyone who wants to learn or have fun with quizzes.
How do I create a quiz?
Just sign up, go to the dashboard, and click on “Create Quiz” to start.
What is the “Do You Know Me?” test?
It’s a fun quiz where you create questions about yourself and share with friends to see who knows you best.
How does Quizify AI work?
Quizify AI helps you generate GK questions and Find who is your real friend automatically based on your topic.
Can I share my quizzes with friends?
Yes, you can share quizzes using a link and track their scores.
What are flashcards and memory cards?
Flashcards help you learn quickly, while memory cards improve recall through repeated practice.
Can I track my score and progress?
Yes, Quizify shows your results, scores, and performance stats.
